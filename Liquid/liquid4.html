CAUTION!!

Crafting robots may prove habit-forming or thought-consuming. Proceed at your own risk.

These robots are formed using polymer clay. To achieve the robotic appearance metallic colors are suggested. Non-metallic samples may be transformed with the addition of a portion of silver, which will preserve the tone of the color. Avoid colors with glitter, but that is determined by personal preference. Stiffer clays are preferred to softer, to preserve detail and maintain shape when adding additional elements. A corollary to this is robot crafting in warm temperatures or with warm hands is more difficult.

For storage purposes, as your squadron gets bigger small shelving units may be required. Desktop organizational units are recommended for the scale of the robots shown here. And sticky tack may be necessary to prevent gravity-induced antenna breakage without the immovability of glue.
